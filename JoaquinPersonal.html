Joaquin Sanchez Garcia

Short CV

My name is Joaquín Sánchez García, I am currently a math phd candidate at University of Toronto under the supervision of Dr. Robert McCann. I studied Applied Mathematics and Actuarial Science at ITAM and a master in science (math) at Uni-Bonn. I also worked in the mexican mayorist energy market for a while at Cfe calificados in the operations team. I am interested in optimal transport in general, stochastic analysis and applications.
